Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about shopping with Corrison. If you can't find the answer you're looking for, please contact us.

To place an order, browse our products and add items to your cart. When you're ready to check out, click on the cart icon in the top right corner, review your items, and click "Proceed to Checkout." Follow the steps to provide shipping and payment information, then submit your order.

We accept all major credit and debit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. We also offer payment through PayPal for your convenience and security.

Yes, we take security very seriously. Our website uses SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We do not store your credit card details on our servers. All payment processing is handled by our secure payment partners.

During checkout, you'll see a field labeled "Coupon Code" or "Discount Code" on the payment page. Enter your code in this field and click "Apply" to see the discount applied to your order before completing your purchase.

Standard shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within the continental United States. Express shipping is available for 1-2 business day delivery. International shipping times vary by location but generally take 7-14 business days.

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Import duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package on the carrier's website. You can also view your order status and tracking information in your account dashboard.

Standard shipping costs $5.00 for all domestic orders. Express shipping is available for $15.00. We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50. International shipping rates vary by destination and will be calculated at checkout.

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be in their original condition with all packaging and tags. Some items, such as personalized products or intimate apparel, may not be eligible for return. Please see our full return policy for details.

To initiate a return, log into your account and go to your order history. Select the order and items you wish to return, then follow the instructions to generate a return shipping label. Pack the items securely in their original packaging and attach the return label. Drop off the package at your nearest postal carrier location.

Once we receive your returned items, it typically takes 3-5 business days to process the return. After processing, refunds will be issued to the original payment method. It may take an additional 3-7 business days for the refund to appear on your account, depending on your payment provider.

If you received a defective or incorrect item, please contact our customer service team immediately. We'll arrange for a return and replacement at no cost to you. Please include photos of the item if possible to help us resolve the issue quickly.

To create an account, click on the "Account" icon in the top right corner of our website and select "Register." Fill in your details, create a password, and submit the form. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Account" icon and select "Login." On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" Enter your email address, and we'll send you instructions to reset your password.

To update your account information, log into your account and go to your profile or account settings. From there, you can edit your personal information, addresses, and communication preferences.

Yes, protecting your personal information is our priority. We use industry-standard security measures and encryption to safeguard your data. We never share your personal information with third parties without your consent. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.
