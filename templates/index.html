Welcome to the Corrison Backend

also known as The "Engine"!

Corrison is a headless, API-powered platform for content and commerce. Powering websites, blogs, eCommerce and more. It provides:

  • Page management: adaptable pages with rich content, hero sections, and CTAs
  • Blog engine: create and schedule blog posts
  • E-commerce: product catalogs, variants, shopping cart, and wishlist
  • Order & payment: shipping, billing, and multiple payment gateway support
  • LinkHub: list a series of links to your content online including video and audio assets
  • Media library: image uploads, asset management, and optimization
  • Headless API: deliver content to any front-end or device
  • Event and Calendar API: (coming soon!) manage events, registrations, and calendar integrations
